Student Examination

Find government benfits and payments available to students and trainees.

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test):

NEET is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). You can find detailed information about the exam, including registration dates, eligibility criteria, exam pattern, and syllabus on the official website.

CAT (Common Admission Test):

CAT is conducted by the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs). Information regarding CAT exam dates, eligibility criteria, registration process, exam pattern, and syllabus can be found on the official CAT website.

SSC (Staff Selection Commission) Exams:

Official Website: https://ssc.nic.in/ SSC conducts various exams like SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC JE, etc. Information regarding exam schedules, eligibility criteria, syllabus, and application process can be found on the official SSC website.

NDA Examination:

UPSC conducts the NDA exam. You can find details on the website, including exam dates, eligibility criteria, syllabus, and application process.

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination):

The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts the JEE Main exam. You can find all the necessary information on the official website, including exam dates, eligibility criteria, syllabus, and application process.
